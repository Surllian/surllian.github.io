Typical Day Of Robots:

Usually, robotics will take care of the job that is repeated, and required more physical power than using logical or intelligence approach. However, some robots can even do some complicated tasks by using the data and experience that they were taught. Nowadays, the use robotic are rapidly increased and widely expand the robotic area. Now, robots can learn how to cook, screw a T-shirt, make sneakers, hotel receptionist, and even conduct a funeral.

Robotic Kitchen, which invented by Moley Robotics, consists of multi-jointed robotic hands filled with a lot of sensors. By using sensors, the Robotic Kitchen can detect the movement of people hand and guest the material to create the dishes. Also, it can download the recipe online by using via apps. By that, a chef master can own a lot of restaurant branches across the world and ensure that the dishes will be identical with their flagships after finishing. Moreover, app developer can develop an app that allow Robotic Kitchen to download famous chef’s recipe by paying the amount of money to support the chiefs, due to the Intellectual property rights. The Moley Robotics claimed that the Robotic Kitchen is released in 2018, however, there is no other announcement about Robotic Kitchen, but it will be costed $75,000.

One of the companies that has evolved the most in this regard is the US Softwear Automation, creator of the Sewbot robotic system, which cuts the fabric and assembles it within the same space. The machine uses cameras and robotic arms that take only four minutes from cutting and sewing the fabric to making the garment. One of the first contracts that it signed was with the US Department of Defense for 1.25 million dollars.

According to the Guardian , Softbank’s humanoid robot ‘Pepper’ dons robes to provide low-cost funerals complete with live-streaming option. In Japan, robots can serve as companions, helpers for the elderly, entertainment bots and even sexual partners, but now Softbank’s humanoid robot Pepper has put itself up for hire as a Buddhist priest for funerals.

Taking the German blessing bot’s idea and running with it, Pepper’s new code will let it chant sutras in a computerized voice while tapping a drum, providing a cheaper alternative to a human priest to see your loved ones off into the eternal sleep. Moreover, this robot can live stream the funeral for relative, who cannot travel to the funeral due to the long distances. However, this robot is not yet to use in a real funeral. But in the future, this robot may be use to perform on a low-cost funeral.

Impact:

There are 3 important impacts of robotics to the society:
First, the impact of structured manipulation. In some industries, especially, car industry, people often use robot to manipulate and combine different part of cars together. This progress required a lot of time, accuracy and man-power. However, with robots, it will reduce the time and money that spent to make a car.

Secondly, the impact of AI structured manipulation. By using machine learning, people can teach robot to do some simple tasks or even complicated jobs. Founded in 2011, Austin, the Briggo has spent total $19 million in funding to develop the first robotic baristas that can make 100 cups of coffee for an hour, this robot allows people to save their favorite drink for next fast transaction and do some advance coffee orders. At this stage, people can use robot as a worker that can memorize some basic things

Finally, the impact of unstructured manipulation, this stage is when robotic is surpass the normal AI robot. When developers create a coffee barista robot, they had taught the robot to work with specific objects. For instance, a coffee cup is one of the specific objects, the developer has to calculate the size of the cup and teach barista robot to learn how to hold a coffee cup or pour the right amount of coffee by recognizing the size of the cup. However, if cup supplier is run out of that cup product, or bankrupted the developers have to teach robots how to use a new coffee cup to avoid the miss calculation when insert a new coffee cup into the robot. In unstructured manipulation, the developers will teach robot to adapt with the situation, which means that the robots are learning automatically. For instance, the Boris 2, the robot can grab object like human, Boris 2 will figure out how to grab an object by using algorithm, it will thing a lot of ways to grab an object using vector and then apply those algorithms to define how to grab an object. Now, robot can be truly assistance for people.

Typical Day Of Cloud:

Cloud allows users to store their data online. They will pay for the amount data that they use to store their information. Compared to normal computer, cloud is very very… very big! It can contain 747 airplanes that fit inside rows and columns. Users can store their information on Cloud for unlimited time (but they have to pay first!). Cloud contains a lot of tools that allows people to freely access with the tools. Users can use Cloud’s tool to support their idea or teach robot how to performed like what they wanted. Cloud’s data center is located everywhere around the World, which means that users can access to Cloud from every countries and every time, but it might take a longer time to pinpoint where the user data is saved. It is safe to say that files and folders are safe when storing in Cloud, it will help people to access and upload their data safe and effectively. Users can use any devices to access to Cloud after sign in to their account, or do some authentication. Moreover, Cloud is a good place to backup user data, the memory storage of Cloud is very big, so user can freely backup their data many times that they wanted after paying an amount of money for their Cloud’s storage. Cloud computing is also used for chat bot’s system, the messages users will respond to the bot, and it will store information on Cloud’s system. There are 4 types of Cloud, private Cloud, public Cloud, hybrid Cloud, and multi Cloud. Private Cloud is a private sever, that is belonged to one individual or organization, the data on that Cloud is only allow that user or organization to access. Public Cloud allows every user to access and download information, any organization can use it. Hybrid Cloud is a mix between private Cloud and public Cloud, which contains private and public servers, it is usually used for company that allow people to access the private cloud after paying an amount of money. For instance, Adobe and Microsoft will allow people to have an activate licenses code after users pay monthly money to extend the licenses code. Multi Cloud is Multi Cloud is the use of multiple cloud computing and storage services in a single heterogeneous architecture.
