As an advocator, it will be better if I can work with the another advocators or mediators. Working with teammates that have same characteristic will be easier. I can freely express my idea about the project and support another teammate in an effective way. Secondly according to the 16Personalities, mediators take pride in being honest and doing the right things in all circumstances.
