Overview:

As an advocator, it will be better if I can work with the another advocators or mediators. Working with teammates that have same characteristic will be easier. I can freely express my idea about the project and support another teammate in an effective way. In addition, When I can freely express my thought about the upcoming project, it makes me feel motivated because my work is meaningful. According to the 16Personalities, the advocate personality prefers not to think themselves as above or below another people and this thought is very important when working in a group, teammates with advocate personality, to be specific, will treat everyone equally and happy to contribute their thought on the group discussion, and it will be easier for a leader to control the group’s moderation and accommodate when conflict happens. Secondly according to the 16Personalities, mediators take pride in being honest and doing the right things in all circumstances. In other words, this characteristic is very necessary when working on the group assignment, it helps people to judge and evaluate the situation to take the right action other than listen to the instruction from another people, they will considerate what is right and wrong and try their best to bring the work to the perfection. Moreover, honest is very importance when participating in group work, an honest teammate will improve the trust between teammates and bring the harmony atmosphere to the group. For those reasons, I prefer to work with advocators and mediators than another type of personality.
